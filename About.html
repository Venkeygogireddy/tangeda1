Discover Tangeda

Tangeda is a village rich in culture and history, known for its heritage and community.

About Tangeda

Tangeda is a heritage-rich village located in [Palnadu District], [Andhra Pradesh], India. Founded during the reign of King Anumula Machireddy Garu, the village is known for its culture, temples, 3 Miles Rock Fort, community festivals, and natural beauty.

Historical Significance

The village has deep historical roots tied to local dynasties, spiritual traditions, and agriculture. Many temples and structures still stand as symbols of our vibrant past.

Demographics

Tangeda has a population of approximately 6000 residents, with schools, a primary health center, water supply, and good road connectivity.

Our Culture

Festivals like Sankranti, Ugadi, Ganesh Chaturthi, Muharram, and Muthyalamma Bonalu are celebrated with great enthusiasm, along with cultural programs organized by youth associations and elders.
