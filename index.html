Discover Tangeda

Tangeda is a village rich in culture and history, known for its heritage and community. Learn about its people, festivals, and the royal roots that shaped it.

Rich Heritage

Founded centuries ago, Tangeda is home to deep-rooted traditions and historic temples that preserve its royal legacy.

Cultural Events

Celebrate colourful festivals like Sankranthi, Ganesh Chaturthi, and Muharram with unity and joy across the village.

Community Services

From schools to healthcare and reliable electricity, the village is equipped to support its people and their future.
